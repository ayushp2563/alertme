Alert Me — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Summary: Alert Me stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. No accounts, no cloud servers, no analytics.

What data is stored

All data is saved in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device:

  • URLs and page titles of sites you choose to watch
  • CSS selectors for section-level watching (if you use that feature)
  • Content hashes and text snapshots used for change detection
  • Change history (timestamps, summaries, diff snippets)
  • Your settings (check frequency, significance threshold)

What data is transmitted

Alert Me makes network requests only to the websites you have explicitly chosen to monitor. These requests are made directly from your browser to those sites — the same as if you visited them yourself. No intermediary servers are involved.

Alert Me does not:

  • Send data to any third-party servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require an account or login
  • Sync data across devices
  • Share your watched sites with other users

Why broad site access is needed

Alert Me requests permission to access websites (<all_urls>) because you can choose to watch any public page. This permission is used solely to:

  • Fetch page content for change comparison
  • Run the element picker when you click "Watch section"

Content scripts are only activated when you explicitly use the picker. Background fetches occur only for URLs you have added to your watch list.

Notifications

Alert Me uses your operating system's native notification API to alert you of detected changes. Notification content is generated locally from diff results and is not sent to any external service.

Data retention and deletion

Data persists until you remove a watched site, clear extension storage, or uninstall the extension. You can delete individual sites and their history from the Dashboard at any time.

Children's privacy

Alert Me does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included in the extension package. Material changes will be noted in the extension changelog.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the extension developer via the project's GitHub repository or support channel listed on the store listing.
