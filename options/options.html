Privacy Policy

Summary: Data stays on your device and in your browser account sync (if enabled). No third-party servers or analytics.

What is stored locally

  • URLs and titles of sites you watch
  • CSS selectors (if using section watch)
  • Content hashes and change history
  • Your settings (frequency, threshold, quiet hours)
  • Cross-device sync data (if enabled) via your browser account

What is transmitted

Network requests are made only to websites you have chosen to monitor — directly from your browser, with no intermediary servers.

Why site access is needed

Broad site access lets you watch any page you choose. Fetches run only for URLs on your watch list. The element picker activates only when you click "Watch section".
